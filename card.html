A layer mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemones's lethal sting.

  • Scientific Name : Amphiprioniae
  • Average Length : 11 centimeters
  • Average LIfespan : 7 years
  • Habitat : Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars,clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reedwellers.They reach about 11 contimeters in length,and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
